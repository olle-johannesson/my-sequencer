KAWAI R-50e SAMPLES from http:/www.abel.c.uk/~maxim/

These samples are direct, unprocessed samples from
the Kawai R-50e Drum Machine. This was a late 80's 
machine with 12-bit 32kHz samples. It was to the 
orignal R-50 what the Alesis HR16b was to the HR16 
- More up-front, pop/dance music sounds, rather than 
trying to emulate an acoustic drum kit. Some of the 
sounds are 'of their era' (especially the Art Of Noise
style orchestra hit), but most are still useful!
